Critical Travel Notes for Seishun 18 Kippu

  • Validation: Present your paper pass to the station staff at the manned gate (not the automatic ticket gates) to get your daily stamp.
  • New Year Congestion: Trains on Jan 1-3 will be crowded near shrines. Aim for the first train of the day to secure a seat.
  • Food Safety: If unsure about ingredients in soups (which may contain alcohol/mirin), stick to plain grilled fish (Shioyaki), Sashimi, or Vegetable Tempura (ask for salt instead of dipping sauce).
  • Safety Buffer: All return journeys arrive at Nippori before 20:30, leaving a 2.5-hour buffer before the last train of the night (approx 23:30).
