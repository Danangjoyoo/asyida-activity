This itinerary maximizes distance each day using only JR local and rapid trains, ensuring same-day return to Nippori Station. Dates are December 31, 2025, to January 4, 2026. Accounts for potential New Year congestion with conservative timings and safety buffers. All JR segments covered by the pass. Prayer scheduled between 11:00-15:00 at acceptable locations. Meals are halal-safe seafood or vegetarian.
